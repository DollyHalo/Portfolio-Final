About Me

Hey There!

With a knack for seeing things from a different perspective, I love creating art using a variety of different mediums.

My name is Holly, but everyone calls me Dolly Halo.

sculpting with clay, wire, metal, cardboard, and tissue paper.

Painting with acrylics, gouache, Indian ink, and spray paint.

Drawing using pencil, colored pencil, micron pens, charcoal, and pastels.

Digital art using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.

Miscellaneous art including but not limited to, photography, collage, scrapbooking, sewing, and embroidery.

I am a full-time student, attending LSU for my BA Art & Digital Art & Web Development.

I'm from a town right outside Chicago, called Hammond, Indiana. In 2014, I moved to southern Louisiana and have been here ever since.

I am a Spinal Cord Injury Survivor. In April 2019, I was in a bad car accident where I broke my spine causing me to become paraplegic from the chest down...
